Ranked 223rd unicorn

Doctolib is a Franco-German company, which is developing an e-health platform. This solution allows patients to make online appointments with general practitioners or specialists, or to take advantage of video teleconsultations.In the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, this system has proven to be particularly relevant and has seen a significant increase in popularity.They are thriving in the health industry. The company has 2 headquarters, one in Berlin and the other in no other than Paris. The service was launched in 2013 in France and 4 years ago in Germany. Today, Doctolib is the first e-health service of Europe with over 50 million visitors every month. According to CBInsights, Doctolib became a unicorn on the 19th of March 2019 and is ranked 223rd.Its valuation is $1.14 billion.
